A note from Andrew
Hope everyone had a great Purim — lots of costumes, good energy, and strong community gatherings across LA.

Passover (Pesach) is only a few weeks away 👀

That means seders, community dinners, learning events, and a lot happening across the city in the lead-up to the holiday. We’re already tracking them in our LA Passover Events Spreadsheet 📆 🍽️, with an interactive Passover Tracker in the works so people can easily find a seder or event that fits their vibe.

In the meantime, here are a few strong events happening this week across the community.
— Andrew
